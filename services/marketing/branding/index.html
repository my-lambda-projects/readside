Logo Design
The Silberman Dental Group

The Silberman Dental Group wanted to attract younger families to their established practice. Their previous logo had traditional fonts with aggressive ALL CAPS that didn’t suit the doctors’ caring nature. The logo also featured a script font, making usage and legibility difficult. It was time to update to a more modern and approachable brand.

Solution:
  • Designer worked collaboratively, presenting various concepts, and creating revisions for a solid final product
  • Curved edges and tilted e provides a fun, quirky quality that works perfectly with the warm personality of the practice
  • Removed ALL CAPS to reveal softer nature, but maintained blue color scheme to work with office colors and offer recognizability
  • Provided logo variations for easier usage (scrubs, signage, letterhead, website, social posts, etc.)

Print Package
Farmer & Williamson

With a successful, established practice in Wichita, Dr. Farmer was expanding and adding an associate, so he wanted to update the practice name to reflect the change and include Dr. Williamson. As such, his logo and stationery all needed to change as well.

Solution:
